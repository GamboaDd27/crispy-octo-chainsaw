New Patients Always Welcome

When looking for a new dentist, it pays to do your homework. As you learn more and compare your options, we believe you will be impressed with what Glisten Dental offers. In fact, you will immediately notice the difference in care and compassion that comes with being treated by our amazing team in our convenient location on Lockwood St. right off of I75.

Our goal is simple. We want to make great dental experiences easy and affordable.

It All Starts with You

You will be amazed at the thoroughness of our new patient exam. At Glisten Dental, this means personal attention from our team of doctors, assistants and hygienists. They will provide you with a complete assessment of your oral health while listening to your concerns and answering your questions. Most importantly, they will develop a plan to return your smile to its optimal condition.

You will receive a complete evaluation of the health of your teeth, gums and jaw. This starts with all necessary digital x-rays and an oral cancer screening. We continue by capturing a series of high definition photos of your mouth – so you can see what we see. And, we evaluate the health of existing crowns, inlays, onlays, dentures or partial dentures.
